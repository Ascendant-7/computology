# Local Setup Guide

1. **Install Flutter:** Ensure your Flutter SDK is installed and configured.
2. **Dependencies:** Run `flutter pub get`.
3. **Configuration:** - Obtain the `firebase_options.dart` from the team lead and place it in `lib/`.
   - Obtain the `google-services.json` (Android) and `GoogleService-Info.plist` (iOS) from the team lead.
   - Place them in their respective `android/app/` and `ios/Runner/` directories.
4. **Run:** `flutter run`
